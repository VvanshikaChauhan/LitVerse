Mystery/Thriller




1.Wrong Place Wrong Time – Gillian McAllister A mother witnesses her teenage son commit a crime, but when she wakes up, it’s the day before the event. As she continues to move back in time, she must uncover the truth before it’s too late. A brilliantly crafted time-loop thriller with emotional depth and stunning twists. 2.The It Girl – Ruth Ware A psychological thriller about a woman haunted by her friend’s murder. When new evidence emerges, she begins to question whether the real killer is still out there. A gripping, suspenseful read with a slow-burn mystery and shocking revelations. 3.Local Woman Missing – Mary Kubica A small town is shaken by multiple disappearances, but when a missing girl returns years later, she brings secrets that could change everything. A tense and unpredictable thriller that keeps you on edge until the last page. 4.None of This is True – Lisa Jewell A woman befriends a true-crime podcaster, only to become the subject of her own terrifying story. With dark twists, unreliable characters, and a haunting premise, this novel is impossible to put down. 5.The Family Upstairs – Lisa Jewell A chilling domestic thriller about inheritance, cult-like families, and deadly secrets that come back to haunt the present. A perfect blend of psychological suspense and dark family drama. 6.The Guest List – Lucy Foley A glamorous wedding on a remote island turns deadly when one guest is murdered. With multiple suspects and hidden motives, this Agatha Christie-style mystery keeps readers hooked till the very end.
