Fantasy




1.Fourth Wing – Rebecca Yarros Set in Basgiath War College, where only the strongest survive, Violet Sorrengail is forced into a world of dragon riders, despite her frail body. With deadly training, war looming, and an enemies-to-lovers romance, this book delivers tension, heartbreak, and thrilling battles. Its rich world-building and gripping pace make it an unmissable fantasy adventure. 2.Empire of the Vampire – Jay Kristoff In a world overrun by darkness, Gabriel de León, the last Silversaint, recounts his life battling the vampire plague. This is a brutal, blood-soaked tale filled with gothic horror, epic battles, and moral dilemmas. The detailed world-building and striking illustrations add to the novel’s intensity, making it a must-read for fans of dark fantasy. 3.The Priory of the Orange Tree – Samantha Shannon A sweeping standalone epic, this novel follows a world divided by ancient rivalries and religion. As an age-old enemy stirs, a queen, a mage, and a dragon rider must navigate political intrigue, magic, and war. Featuring powerful female protagonists, breathtaking world-building, and a fresh take on dragons, it’s a modern fantasy masterpiece. 4.The Atlas Six – Olivie Blake Six of the world’s most powerful magicians are invited to join a secret society that controls knowledge. But with only five spots available, they must outwit each other to secure their place. A blend of dark academia, philosophy, and complex characters, this book is a mind-bending experience filled with tension and betrayal. 5.Daughter of the Moon Goddess – Sue Lynn Tan Inspired by Chinese mythology, this novel follows Xingyin, the daughter of the moon goddess, as she embarks on a quest to free her mother from imprisonment. Along the way, she trains as a warrior, faces political conflicts, and battles forbidden love. With lyrical writing and a stunning magical setting, this book is a beautiful fantasy adventure. 6.Legends & Lattes – Travis Baldree A cozy, heartwarming fantasy about an orc warrior who retires from battle to open a coffee shop. As she builds her dream business, she discovers unexpected friendships, romance, and a new sense of belonging. A charming and wholesome read for fantasy lovers looking for something different.
