"Fourth Wing" – Rebecca Yarros (Fantasy/Romance) Violet Sorrengail was never meant to be a dragon rider, but when she’s forced into Basgiath War College, survival becomes her only goal. With deadly training, ruthless competitors, and the constant risk of being burned alive, she must prove her worth—or die trying. As she navigates betrayals, unexpected alliances, and a slow-burning romance, Violet realizes she may be more powerful than anyone expected. A perfect mix of action, adventure, and emotional depth, Fourth Wing has captivated fantasy readers worldwide.





"The Crash" – Freida McFadden (Mystery/Thriller) A woman survives a horrific car crash, only to wake up with no memory of how she got there. As she starts unraveling the events leading up to the accident, unsettling details emerge—was it really an accident, or something far more sinister? As paranoia sets in, secrets come to light, and she must figure out the truth before it's too late. Full of unexpected twists and gripping suspense, The Crash is an unputdownable psychological thriller that keeps readers guessing until the final page.





"The Let Them Theory" – Mel Robbins (Non-Fiction/Self-Help) In this empowering book, Mel Robbins introduces a simple mindset shift—let them. Instead of forcing people to change or seeking validation, she argues that true freedom comes from accepting what you can't control. Whether in relationships, friendships, or career decisions, the Let Them Theory helps readers set boundaries, reduce stress, and embrace a healthier, more fulfilling life. Packed with real-life examples and practical advice, this book is a game-changer for anyone struggling with control, expectations, or emotional well-being.
