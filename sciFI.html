Science Fiction




1.The Measure – Nikki Erlick One morning, every person receives a mysterious box containing a string that reveals how long they have left to live. As the world grapples with this knowledge, individuals must decide how to live their remaining days. A thought-provoking, emotional exploration of fate and free will. 2.Upgrade – Blake Crouch A scientist wakes up to discover his DNA has been altered, making him the next stage of human evolution. But with his newfound abilities comes danger, as powerful forces seek to control him. A fast-paced sci-fi thriller packed with ethical dilemmas and action. 3.Sea of Tranquility – Emily St. John Mandel A hauntingly beautiful time-travel novel that explores human connection across centuries, weaving together different timelines in an unforgettable way. 4.Dead Silence – S.A. Barnes A sci-fi horror novel about a salvage crew that stumbles upon a long-lost luxury spaceship, only to find something sinister lurking inside. A chilling mix of psychological suspense and space horror. 5.The Space Between Worlds – Micaiah Johnson In a future where multiverse travel is possible, a woman who can only visit worlds where her other self is dead discovers a conspiracy that threatens all realities. A fascinating blend of sci-fi, identity, and power. 6.Klara and the Sun – Kazuo Ishiguro A beautifully written AI-driven novel about love, loneliness, and what it means to be human, told from the perspective of an artificial friend observing the world.
