BOOK OF THE MONTH – MARCH 2025 "Broken Country" by Clare Leslie Hall ABOUT THE AUTHOR – CLARE LESLIE HALL Clare Leslie Hall is a renowned historical fiction writer known for her deeply emotional and thought-provoking storytelling. With a strong background in literature and history, she creates immersive narratives that transport readers to different eras. Her previous works have received critical acclaim, making her one of the most compelling voices in contemporary fiction. ABOUT THE BOOK – "BROKEN COUNTRY" Set in the 1950s and 1960s, Broken Country is a powerful story about love, loss, and the weight of unspoken truths. The novel follows Beth, a woman who unexpectedly reconnects with her first love, Gabriel. As their paths cross once again, buried memories resurface, forcing them to confront the past. The book beautifully captures the struggles of time, personal choices, and the emotional bonds that shape our lives. WHAT READERS ARE SAYING "A deeply moving novel that lingers in the mind long after the final page." "Clare Leslie Hall’s writing is poetic and profound, bringing the past to life with remarkable depth." "A captivating story filled with nostalgia, heartbreak, and hope." Both critics and readers have praised Broken Country for its rich storytelling, complex characters, and historical depth. RELEASE DATE AND CURRENT SUCCESS Released: March 2025 Bestseller Status: The novel is already gaining recognition as a top historical fiction pick of the year. Featured in major book clubs, it continues to receive widespread acclaim from readers and literary critics. WHY READ IT For those who appreciate deeply emotional and well-crafted historical fiction, Broken Country is a must-read. The novel’s immersive storytelling, layered emotions, and beautifully written prose make it an unforgettable experience. Discover the story for yourself.
