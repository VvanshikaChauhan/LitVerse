Romance




1.Before We Were Strangers – Renée Carlino A moving second-chance romance that spans years of longing and missed opportunities. Matt and Grace shared a deep love during their college days, but life pulled them apart. When they unexpectedly cross paths again, they must decide whether to risk their hearts once more. Filled with nostalgia, passion, and heartfelt moments, this book is a beautifully written love story. 2.Every Summer After – Carley Fortune Persephone and Sam spent six unforgettable summers together at the lake, but one mistake changed everything. Years later, fate brings them back together, forcing them to confront their past. This emotionally charged novel is a beautiful blend of first love, heartbreak, and redemption, making it perfect for fans of bittersweet romance. 3.Love and Other Words – Christina Lauren A childhood friendship that turned into love—until a tragic misunderstanding tore them apart. Macy and Elliot reunite years later, leading to a deeply emotional journey filled with regret, love, and second chances. Told in alternating timelines, this novel explores the impact of words and memories on our lives. 4.Icebreaker – Hannah Grace A sizzling romance between a figure skater and a hockey player forced to train together. What starts as rivalry soon turns into undeniable chemistry, making for a fun, swoon-worthy read. With a mix of sports, banter, and steamy romance, it’s a perfect pick for those who love competitive love stories. 5.The Spanish Love Deception – Elena Armas Catalina desperately needs a fake boyfriend for her sister’s wedding in Spain. Enter her infuriating coworker, Aaron Blackford, who volunteers for the role. What follows is a slow-burn romance filled with witty dialogue, forced proximity, and heartwarming moments. A perfect rom-com for fans of fake-dating tropes. 6.Happy Place – Emily Henry A deeply emotional yet charming story about exes forced to pretend they’re still together during a friend’s vacation. As old feelings resurface, they must confront what went wrong in their relationship. Emily Henry delivers another heartfelt novel with humor, warmth, and an exploration of love’s complexities.
